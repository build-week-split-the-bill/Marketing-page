Split The Bill

Login and fill in the blanks and start Splitting The Bill with the group instead of arguing who had what. We developed this application so that the fun could continue even after the check came. So don't fret any longer when the waiter/waitress comes bearing the check of bad news... instead open up Split The Bill and make this a seamlessly simple process. You and your fiends will have no need to banter (except maybe who actually ordered the breadsticks! lol...).

Our Mission

We’re here to make moving money easy. Stop fighting over who paid what and skip the awkward conversation. Our mission is to help you keep the peace and split the cost of the event or “hang out” in a format that is easy to use and pleasing to the eye.
