Login and fill in the blanks and start Splitting The Bill with the group instead of arguing who had what. We developed this application so that the fun could continue even after the check came. Our Mission

We’re here to make moving money easy. Stop fighting over who paid what and skip the awkward conversation. Our mission is to help you keep the peace and split the cost of the event or “hang out” in a format that is easy to use and pleasing to the eye.

How It Works

Split The Bill uses a person to person money exchange that removes the middle man. Makes paying your friends for your part of the group bill more secure and without the need to carry all that pesky money you have...lol.
